JSS Level (Grade 6–7): Number Sense, Algebra Starter & Geometry

Core foundations for building robust operations, metric patterns, measurements, and shapes.

Topic 1

Integers, Decimals & Fractions (Core)

  • ✓ Integers: Operations, ordering on a number line, interpretation of negatives.
  • ✓ Fractions: Simplifying, equivalent conversions, and addition/subtraction.
  • ✓ Decimals: Place values, rounding, converting cleanly to fractions.
  • ✓ Percentages: Solving part-of-100 values & simple word problems.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Compute using correct BODMAS sequence: $15 + 4 \times (12 - 7) \div 2$"

1. Parantheses first: $12 - 7 = 5$

2. Multiplication/Division left-to-right: $4 \times 5 = 20$, then $20 \div 2 = 10$

3. Addition last: $15 + 10 = \mathbf{25}$

Topic 2

Ratio, Rate & Proportion

  • ✓ Ratio: Simplifying ratios; sharing total quantities in given ratios.
  • ✓ Rate: Understanding units like $km/h$, $m/s$, and real-life resource sharing.
  • ✓ Proportion: Direct proportion problems and progressive variable analysis.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Divide KES 4,500 in the ratio $2:3$."

1. Find total parts: $2 + 3 = 5\text{ parts}$

2. Calculate value of one part: $4,500 \div 5 = 900\text{ KES}$

3. Distribute shares: $2 \times 900 = \mathbf{1,800\text{ KES}}$ and $3 \times 900 = \mathbf{2,700\text{ KES}}$

Topic 3

Algebra Starter: Expressions & Equations

  • ✓ Algebraic expressions: Grouping like terms and expanding single bracket expressions.
  • ✓ Substitution: Correctly evaluating algebraic statements given specific values.
  • ✓ Equations: Solving simple single-variable linear balance equations.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Solve for $x$: $3x - 7 = 14$"

1. Add $7$ to both sides: $3x = 14 + 7 \implies 3x = 21$

2. Divide both sides by $3$: $x = 21 \div 3 \implies \mathbf{x = 7}$

Topic 4

Geometry & Mensuration: Lines, Angles & Shapes

  • ✓ Angles: Properties of triangles and basic parallel line angle configurations.
  • ✓ Mensuration: Accurately calculating area & perimeter of rectangles and triangles.
  • ✓ Circles: Intro to radius, diameter, and simple properties.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Calculate the area of a triangle with a base of $12\text{ cm}$ and a vertical height of $8\text{ cm}$."

1. Formula: $\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height}$

2. Substitute values: $\text{Area} = \frac{1}{2} \times 12 \times 8$

3. Calculate: $6 \times 8 = \mathbf{48\text{ cm}^2}$

JSS Level (Grade 8–9): Algebra Expansion, Trig Intro & Mensuration

Advance from basic arithmetic to algebraic expansion, statistical charting, and angular systems.

Topic 1

Expanding & Factorizing

  • ✓ Expansion: Distributive laws over double brackets & expressions.
  • ✓ Factorization: Taking out highest common factors (HCF) and grouping.
  • ✓ Simultaneous Equations: Elimination and substitution methods.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Expand and simplify: $(x + 3)(x - 5)$"

1. Use FOIL Method: $x \cdot x - 5 \cdot x + 3 \cdot x - 15$

2. Combine like terms: $-5x + 3x = -2x$

3. Answer: $\mathbf{x^2 - 2x - 15}$

Topic 2

Indices & Standard Form

  • ✓ Laws of Indices: Multiplication, division, and power properties.
  • ✓ Standard Form: Representing extremely large or small numbers beautifully.
  • ✓ Surds: Simplifying radical forms and rationalizing.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Express $345,000$ in standard form."

1. Move the decimal point to get a number between $1$ and $10$: $3.45$

2. Count the shifts to the left: $5$ spaces.

3. Answer: $\mathbf{3.45 \times 10^5}$

Topic 3

Advanced Geometry & 3D Mensuration

  • ✓ 3D Shapes: Computing surface area and volume of cylinders & prisms.
  • ✓ Coordinate Geometry: Finding distances and midpoints between two coordinate pairs.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Calculate the volume of a cylinder with radius $7\text{ cm}$ and height $10\text{ cm}$ (Take $\pi = \frac{22}{7}$)"

1. Formula: $V = \pi r^2 h$

2. Substitution: $V = \frac{22}{7} \times 7 \times 7 \times 10$

3. Simplify: $22 \times 7 \times 10 = \mathbf{1,540\text{ cm}^3}$

Topic 4

Statistics & Probability

  • ✓ Central Tendency: Understanding mean, median, and mode metrics.
  • ✓ Graphical Representation: Interpretation of pie charts and line grids.
  • ✓ Probability: Sample spaces and predicting chance outcomes.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Find the median of this data set: $4, 8, 3, 12, 10, 5, 9$"

1. Sort the numbers chronologically: $3, 4, 5, \mathbf{8}, 9, 10, 12$

2. Locate the central value (4th number out of 7): $\mathbf{8}$

Senior Secondary Entry (Grade 10 / Form 4): Exam Mastery

Accelerated checklist of high-yield revision nodes including quadratic formulas, coordinate graphs, and trigonometry.

⚠️ Exam Strategy Alert:

Always write down your known values, declare the target formula first, compute carefully, and double-check calculator settings (Degrees vs Radians) during trigonometry tests!

Unit 1

Functions & Quadratic Equations

  • ✓ Quadratic Equation: Mastery of $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ solving using factorization & formula.
  • ✓ Functional Domain: Identifying domains, ranges, and graphical transformations.

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Solve for $x$: $x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0$"

1. Find two numbers that multiply to $6$ and add up to $-5$: these are $-2$ and $-3$.

2. Express as factors: $(x - 2)(x - 3) = 0$

3. Solve for $x$: $\mathbf{x = 2}$ or $\mathbf{x = 3}$

Unit 2

Trigonometry & Coordinate Lines

  • ✓ Straight Lines: Equation formulas like $y = mx + c$, gradients, and intercepts.
  • ✓ Trigonometry ratios: Applying Sine, Cosine, and Tangent rules (SOHCAHTOA).

Practice Prompt & Drill

"Find the gradient of a straight line passing through points $(2, 3)$ and $(5, 9)$."

1. Formula: $m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}$

2. Substitution: $m = \frac{9 - 3}{5 - 2} = \frac{6}{3}$

3. Answer: $\mathbf{m = 2}$

About Dominic

Dominic Mutuku Mutua is a dedicated educationist, researcher, and digital service provider born in Machakos and raised in Kalawa Ward, Makueni County. Currently based in Nairobi and serving under the Teachers Service Commission (TSC), he brings a strong blend of academic excellence and practical leadership.

Dominic holds an MSc. in ICT Integration in Education and Instructional Design from JKUAT and an M.Ed. in Advanced Teaching from the University of the People (USA). With a solid foundation in Mathematics, Physics, and technology, he is deeply committed to delivering practical, teacher-centered solutions.

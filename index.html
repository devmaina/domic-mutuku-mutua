About

Dominic Mutuku Mutua is a dedicated educationist, researcher, and digital service provider born in Machakos and raised in Kalawa Ward, Mbooni East Sub-county, Makueni County. Currently based in Nairobi and serving under the Teachers Service Commission (TSC), he brings a strong blend of academic excellence and practical experience. Dominic holds a Master of Science in ICT Integration in Education and Instructional Design from JKUAT and a Master of Education in Advanced Teaching from the University of the People (USA). With a solid foundation in Mathematics, Physics, and technology, he is deeply committed to delivering practical, member-centered solutions in every institution he serves.

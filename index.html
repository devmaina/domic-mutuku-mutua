About Dominic Mutuku Mutua

Dominic Mutuku Mutua is a dynamic and passionate educator, widely recognized as a pioneer in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) education in Kenya. With a profound commitment to nurturing the next generation of innovators, he has dedicated his career to empowering students with critical thinking skills and a love for technological advancement.

Currently, Dominic serves as a highly respected teacher at **St. Anna's Girls High School in Nairobi**, where he inspires young women to break barriers in STEM fields. His teaching methodologies are known for being engaging, hands-on, and forward-thinking, preparing students not just for exams, but for real-world challenges.

Beyond the classroom, Dominic is a fervent advocate for educational reform and technological integration. He believes in creating accessible and revolutionary learning experiences for all. His vision extends to shaping the future of education in Kenya and beyond.

Empowering Teachers, Shaping the Future of Education

Dominic Mutuku Mutua is also a strong advocate for teachers' welfare and professional development. He is currently vying for the prestigious post of **Organising Secretary for KUPPET (Kenya Union of Post Primary Education Teachers)**. His campaign focuses on:

  • check_circle Advocating for improved working conditions and remuneration for teachers.
  • check_circle Enhancing professional development opportunities, especially in STEM and Ed-Tech.
  • check_circle Promoting ethical leadership and transparency within the union.
  • check_circle Fostering collaboration and innovation among educators.
